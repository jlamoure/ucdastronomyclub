Welcome to the new astronomy club website! We hold different events and activities throughout the year, all of which are open to the public.

Our events are put on by the Astronomy Club officers and if you'd like to help plan or have some input or ideas, please come to our club meetings, which will be announced on the facebook page or the news page, which gets updates from the club email list. Elections for officers are at the end of Spring Quarter.
